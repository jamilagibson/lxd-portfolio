About
Me

Pedagogy Design Systems Biliteracy AI Authoring Accessibility

I channel my passion for learning design into instructional experiences that balance rigorous pedagogy and intuitive, user-centered design.

My approach brings together three disciplines: learning science (what makes instruction effective), product design (how to build experiences that are intuitive and accessible), and systems thinking (collaborating seamlessly with engineering teams), using rigorous proficiency, design systems thinking, and deep K–12 expertise to every project.

At McGraw Hill, I translate complex Spanish-language instructional content into scalable, component-based digital learning for K–5 bilingual learners nationwide. I was promoted from Associate Instructional Designer to Instructional Designer in October 2025.
